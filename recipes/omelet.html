Omelet

Meet the keto omelette that is my new favourite meal in under 30 minutes. Easy, delicious and less than 6 ingredients.


Ingredients

Only 6 ingredients in our rolled low carb omelette. I have kept it basic but you can fill with your favourite fillings.

  • Eggs- I use jumbo eggs from cage free pasture raised hens.
  • Swiss cheese- you can use any of your favourite cheeses.
  • Leg ham- substitute any protein or vegetables you prefer. Layer thinly so the omelette easy to roll.

Step by step innstructions

easy to follow - let's dive into it:

  1. Add the eggs, sour cream, parmesan and seasoning to a large bowl and whisk until combined.
  2. Pour the egg mixture into a lined baking tray and bake for 10 minutes in a fan forced oven at 180Â°C.
  3. Turn the baked omelette out onto a silicone mat and layer with cheese and ham.
  4. Roll the filled omelette into roll lengthwise. Bake for a further 5 minutes to heat and melt the cheese. Serve hot or cold.
